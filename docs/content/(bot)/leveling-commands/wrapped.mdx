### Description

This command generates an image showing your Lurkr year in review. It displays your leveling statistics across all of
your Lurkr-enabled servers, giving you a complete overview of your activity throughout the year.

The generated image includes:

- Your most active month of the year
- The server where you were most active
- Various other leveling statistics and highlights

You can share your Lurkr Wrapped image with friends or the people you chat with the most!

<Callout type="info">This command is only available during December and January.</Callout>

### Command Structure

```
/wrapped
```

### Permission

- N/A **(User)**
- `Attach Files` **(Bot)**
